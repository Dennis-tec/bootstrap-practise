About

Maasai Mara Game Reserve is located at the border of Kenya and Tanzania. Curently , it's the lagest National Reserve in the country. In relation to the variety of wild animals in the area with the big five included, approximately 5000 foreign and domestic visitors visits the Mara everyday. Statistics from the Kenyan national government and the county government of Narok shows that the game reserve is the most income generating tourist activity in the country.
